7 Types of Digital Marketing in the Business World

There are so many types of digital marketing that business and business travelers need to know. The following are several types of digital marketing media that you can implement in your business so that they are better known and increase company turnover

1. Social Media Marketing or SMM

The first type of digital marketing is Social Media Marketing whose use refers to social media platforms. Through this medium you can establish good and harmonious relationships with clients and potential customers of your business. In running an advertising system through social media, you must pay attention to every uploaded content so that it becomes attractive. Several social media platforms that advertisers can choose from include Instagram, Whatsapp, Facebook, and so on.

2. Content Marketing

As a type of digital marketing, content marketing focuses on creating and distributing content that aims to target markets or consumers. So that this can provide benefits from targeted consumers. Examples of content marketing include blog posts, videos, podcasts, case studies, e-books, and other content that attracts visitors. However, you must make sure that the contents are made in accordance with the products or services offered.

3. Pay - Per - Click (PPC)

Pay per click is a marketing model like Google Adwords that some business people may have heard of. In using this method, advertisers pay visitors who open their website. So that profits can be obtained by publishers / publishers through the large number of clicks. You can set the rate per click yourself or follow the recommendations given by the advertiser. Know that the higher the cost per click on the website, the greater your website ad will appear on the top page and be seen by more Google users.

4. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate Marketing is a type of marketing through digital media, in which advertisers establish business partnerships with business actors. The goal is to get commissions for the business traffic you provide and increase business turnover. In this marketing, you only need to share links to business relations websites or purchasing websites. Furthermore, profits or commissions for advertisers will be obtained through link clicks made by visitors until a purchase transaction occurs. So a marketing strategy like this is perfect for bloggers, content creators, YouTubers, and other content creators. But most importantly, don't forget to include affiliate links that will be advertised in each of your content to make it easier for targets or visitors to click.

5. Email Marketing

Email marketing is one of the most popular digital marketing methods, where marketing uses email. Email is a promotional medium for a product or service, the email has a function to establish relationships with consumers. In addition, this promotional media also functions to provide information related to the advertised product or service. In short, the email that you send to customers is not just a message of social interaction or random information. But in the email you have to give an interesting impression regarding the product or service that you offer for a business, thus increasing the purchasing power of potential customers. This is one of the benefits of marketing media via email.

6. Search Engine Optimization or SEO

SEO or Search Engine Optimization is a marketing strategy to get high quality traffic, which can be obtained for free or for a fee. Previously, you must first recognize what the real purpose of marketing through SEO is . If your goal is to attract lots of visitors, it's best to optimize your content by determining relevant keywords. Make sure you are relevant in including the title, description, website content, and backlinks used in advertising so that they are easier to find.

7. Search Engine Marketing or SEM

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a business marketing strategy that is often chosen to increase website visibility in search engine results or SERPs. Some commonly used search engines such as Google, Yahoo, and Bing. Search Engine Marketing is also commonly referred to as paid search or Paid Search, paid clicks, or paid search advertising. You only need to bid on keywords that visitors often use in searching for a desired product or service. By choosing the right keywords, of course the chance for your content to be known and seen by many visitors will also be greater. So that when visitors have typed keywords and appear in search results, your ad will appear on the search page. Digital marketing has indeed become one of the effective marketing media carried out by business people or business actors. There are many types of digital marketing that can be chosen and adjusted to the target market and type of business.
